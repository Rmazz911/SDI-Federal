About SDI Federal

Building the connections and infrastructure that strengthen America.

SDI Federal, LLC is a national strategic advisory and industrial development firm dedicated to strengthening America’s Defense Industrial Base, expanding domestic manufacturing, and accelerating innovation across the federal, defense, academic, healthcare, and commercial sectors.

We help organizations navigate the complex intersection of government, industry, advanced manufacturing, workforce development, technology commercialization, industrial infrastructure, and economic development.

Unlike traditional consulting firms that provide recommendations alone, SDI Federal works alongside clients to transform ideas into executable strategies, strategic partnerships, and measurable outcomes.

A National Network

Connected across government, industry, academia, and infrastructure.

SDI Federal’s leadership is supported by decades of collaboration across the federal innovation ecosystem and the Defense Industrial Base.

Through Robert Mazzuca’s role as Executive Director of the SMART Congressional Initiative, SDI Federal operates within a broad network spanning congressional leaders, federal agencies, defense organizations, universities, national laboratories, manufacturers, technology companies, workforce organizations, and economic development partners.

The SMART Congressional Initiative

SMART is a bipartisan federal collaboration founded by the President’s Science Advisor and Director of the White House Office of Science and Technology Policy, working in partnership with the Initiative’s congressional founders.

This network provides SDI Federal with a unique ability to connect organizations, align strategic interests, and build partnerships that move innovation, manufacturing, and economic development initiatives forward.
